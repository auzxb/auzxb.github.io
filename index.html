Xiaobin Zhuang   庄晓滨

My name is Zhuang Xiaobin and I am currently working on multi-modal audio generation at Tencent Music Entertainment Group.

I have extensive experience in audio generation, including speech synthesis, singing voice synthesis, voice cloning, and video sound effects generation. In my previous career, I also gained experience in singing voice separation, music recognition, audio processing, and image generation. I am familiar with various generative techniques such as diffusion models and generative adversarial networks, and have a good background in multi-modal understanding and generation.

I focus on the development of the state-of-the-art models in business operations, all with the goal of accelerating the development of the artificial intelligence industry.
